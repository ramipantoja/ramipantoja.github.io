rasp films is a british independent film production company, bafta nominated in 2021 and currently developing a slate of stylised commercial feature films.

our mission is to produce visually striking and genre-defying films that push the boundaries of traditional storytelling. we strive to create entertaining and thought-provoking films that challenge audiences' expectations and leave a lasting impact. we are dedicated to making the filmmaking process fun, creative, and inclusive for everyone involved.
